History

Gentrade Global was established by individuals who are passionate about helping and encouraging small hold farmers, and processors in West Africa become more active through ensuring they have access to international value-added markets.

Many cocoa farmers in West Africa still work with very traditional methods and lack the knowledge and understanding of modern best practices, which could lead to significant improvements in the quality of the cocoa they produce and indeed greater yields.

We identified the challenges faced by exporters from developing countries in complying with strict export market requirement demands on production and quality from importing countries.

This inspired us to launch an agency that would support and communicate to the farmers the industry needs on good agricultural practices and quality control measures while harvesting.

In return, the farmers and exporters will be better prepared for export and meet consumer demands while complying with quality specification in the commodity market.

Our goal is to enable farmers achieve better incomes and living standards and to help create thriving communities that are armed with the social services, related to education health and nutrition
