Vision And Values

OUR VISION

Gentrade global is the link between the producer, processor, the exporter and the buyer aiming to play a leading role in the successful transaction of goods between the two entities.

Our aim is to empower farmers and their communities to help them become entrepreneurs who manage professional and responsible farms that generate a living income.

Everything Gentrade global does is deeply rooted in our 3-core values- Integrity, transparency, and social responsibility.


OUR VALUES

Integrity

We have strong ethical and moral principles and are committed to doing what is right through building trust with customers, suppliers, and the communities we work with.

Transparency

We communicate openly and sincerely in all our engagements, building strong relationships all across the value chain. Our supply chains cover origination, primary processing, and export, and merchandising globally.

Social Responsibility

Our environment plays an integral part on how we survive. We conduct ourselves in an ethical manner ensuring we and the farmers we work with abide by the best practices. We ensure our business practices are friendly towards the environment and strictly comply with all local environmental laws and regulations
