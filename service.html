Service

Consulting

There are many aspects to consider when embarking on international trade such as import export laws and regulation, trade policies, operations, logistics arrangements, and export procedures along with many other issues that can complicate your import and export process. Gentrade Global works with importers/exporters by advising them on the necessary documentations and up to date information for exporting and importing their products and everything else that goes into making a profitable import and export deal materialize. Our services address all the challenges that your business may encounter during the process of importing and exporting.



Agri-Commodity Trading

We work with small hold farmers and processing companies specializing in the raw and processed goods of cocoa, cashew and coffee, playing a strategic role of an international commodity agent in major producing West African countries with ancillary agricultural operations in specialty commodities. This ensures we save our clients the time and effort required when sourcing.
