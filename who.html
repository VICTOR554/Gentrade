Who We are

We are an international trade consultancy and agency serving the agricultural commodity industry on the global market.

We advice our clients on the best strategy when entering into new international market unknown to them, where they are able to market their products while reducing the risks associated with conducting business in unfamiliar overseas markets.

As intermediaries between exporters from West Africa and international buyers we help our clients establish connections in foreign countries.

With our strong international expertise in the agricultural commodity industry, we are able provide insight and guidance on all international business activities, providing our clients with the chance to operate on the global markets with mitigated risk.

The buying or selling of goods on the international market can be complex, time consuming, and sometimes involves risk due to the lack of distance and other factors unforeseen, this is where we mediate, therefore avoiding unnecessary oversights.

Our head office is based in the United Kingdom with offices across West Africa.
